Common Questions

My companion isn't responding

BareClaw needs a Claude API key to power conversations. Go to Settings → AI Engine, paste your key, and tap Save & Activate. Get a free key at console.anthropic.com.

Her Mode / Him Mode isn't listening

Make sure the mode is toggled on from the second tab. The app will ask for Microphone and Speech Recognition permissions — both are required. You can check these in iOS Settings → BareClaw.

How do I change my companion's photo?

In the Chat tab, tap your companion's name and photo in the top-left corner. Choose Change Photo to pick an image from your library.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored on your device. To remove everything permanently, go to iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → BareClaw → Delete App.

The app says my API key was rejected

This usually means the key is correct but your Anthropic account has no credits. Visit console.anthropic.com to add credits, then return to Settings and tap Refresh Claude Status.

How do I add a voice?

Go to Settings → Voice. You can use the built-in system voice for free, or add an ElevenLabs API key for a neural voice. ElevenLabs has a free tier at elevenlabs.io.
