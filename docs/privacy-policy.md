# BareClaw Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 2, 2026
**Developer:** Michael Valasek

---

## The short version

BareClaw keeps your data on your device. There is no BareClaw account, no BareClaw server, and no advertising. If you choose to connect Claude or ElevenLabs, your messages go to those providers through your own API key — not through us.

---

## What BareClaw collects and where it lives

### On your device (never leaves unless you choose)

| Data | What it is | Where it's stored |
|---|---|---|
| Your first name | Entered during onboarding | Local UserDefaults |
| Companion choice and bond score | Which companion you use, your score | Local UserDefaults |
| Chat history | Conversations with your companion | Local device storage |
| Dream journal entries | Dreams and reflections you log | Local device storage |
| Interests | Topics you select or add | Local UserDefaults |
| Companion portrait photo | Optional photo you choose from your library | Local Documents folder |
| Memory entries | Hermes learning engine summaries | Local device storage |
| App settings | Voice preferences, mode settings | Local UserDefaults |

### Through your own third-party accounts (optional, user-controlled)

If you add your own API key in Settings, messages are sent to that provider under your account:

| Provider | What is sent | When |
|---|---|---|
| Anthropic (Claude) | Your chat messages and companion context | Each time you send a message |
| ElevenLabs | Companion response text for voice synthesis | When voice is enabled |

BareClaw does not receive, store, or have access to any data sent to these providers. You are the account holder. Their privacy policies govern that data.

### Microphone and speech (Him/Her Mode only)

If you activate Him/Her Mode, BareClaw uses your microphone and Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework to listen for your companion's wake word and spoken input. This audio is processed on-device by Apple — it is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by BareClaw. You can pause or deactivate Him/Her Mode at any time.

---

## What BareClaw does NOT do

- No BareClaw account or sign-in required
- No BareClaw analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising or ad networks
- No sale or sharing of your data with data brokers
- No tracking across other apps or websites
- No push notifications unless you enable them for reminders you configure

---

## Children

BareClaw is rated 12+ on the App Store. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## Data retention and deletion

All data is on your device. To delete it, delete the app. Companion portrait photos are stored in the app's Documents folder and are removed when the app is deleted. Chat history, memories, journal entries, and settings are stored in app-sandboxed storage and are also removed on app deletion.

If you used a third-party provider (Claude, ElevenLabs), contact that provider directly to request deletion of any data processed under your account.

---

## Security

Your data never leaves your device through BareClaw infrastructure. API keys you enter are stored in local UserDefaults within the app sandbox, accessible only to BareClaw on your device.
